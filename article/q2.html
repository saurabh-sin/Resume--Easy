Can I change the language of my resume?

You can change the language of your resume or cover letter anytime. We currently support English, Русский, Nederlands, Español, Svenska, Dansk, Norsk, Deutsch, Français, Português, Italiano, Polski, čeština, Suomi.

Change the Language of Your Resume or Cover Letter

Click in to change the language of your resume by clicking the flag at the top of the editor and selecting your language. See the screenshot below.

You can change the language of your resume in two quick steps using the new version of our resume editor.

Step 1: Make sure you're using the newest version of the resume editor by confirming your app looks like the screenshot below. If your app looks different, you can click this link to update your version**:https://resumeeasy/app/create-resume/templates

Step 2: Click in to edit the resume you want to change the language for. Change the language of your resume by clicking the flag at the top of the editor and selecting your language. See the screenshot below.
